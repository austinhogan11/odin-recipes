Mojito

Description

This simple yet refreshing drink is great for your summer days by the pool.

Ingridients

  • Rum: A white rum of your choice
  • Sparkling soda: La Croix or your choice of sparkling soda
  • Mint: Spearmint which is what you'll often find in grocery stores
  • Lime: Freshly squeezed lime juice
  • Sugar: No simple syrup here, granulated sugar.

Steps

  1. Place mint leaves & lime in glass. Use muddler & crush to release mint oils & lime juice.
  2. Add remaining lime wedges & 2 tablespoons sugar. Muddle again to release ime juice.
  3. Fill glass almost to the top with ice.
  4. Pour in rum.
  5. Fill glass with club soda.
  6. Stir to mix.
