Spicy Marg

Description

When you need some kick, try this spicy & skinny margarita

Ingridients

  • Tequila: This recipe calls for silver Patron but any silver tequila will work.
  • Lime Juice: FRESH juice is a must.
  • Orange Juice: A little sweetness to balance out the spicy.
  • Jalapeno: The secret ingridient that gives the marg its kick.
  • Salt + Lime Wedge: Salt + Lime for asthetics. For a twsit subsitute salt for Tajin spice.

Steps

  1. Rim the glass & fill with ice.
  2. Add marg ingridients & ice to a cocktail shaker.
  3. Shake for 30s then strain into the glass.
