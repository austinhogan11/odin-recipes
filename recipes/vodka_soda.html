Vodka Soda

Description

Master this simple & classic cocktail to server to your next guests!

Ingridients

  • La Croix: 4oz of your choice of Sparkling Soda
  • Titos Vodka: 1oz of your choice of vodka
  • 1 Lime Wedge: A lime for asthetics

Steps

  1. Fill a glass with ice.
  2. Pour vodka & soda water over the ice.
  3. Stir to combine.
  4. Squeeze in lime edge.
